National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences

The National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences main purpose is to find the impact of the environment on humans and does alot of work with the research of plant based medicine. They have been crutial in the reseach of many types of medicine in the past including medical marijuana. They have also done work to prove substances, like chromium 6, can cause cancer within lab aniamls and humans. Another major accomplishment they made was being able to help scientists find a way to stop testing on animals and start doing cell based tests for medicine like vaccines and antibiotics that help fight off illnesses. In 2011 they fought to add 8 substances to the national list of carcinages. This is a list of substances that can be harmful or even deadly to the human body. In 2014 they formulated a list of 243 substances that are known to cause cancer within the human body. They are continuously working to assure that humans are health and safe.
